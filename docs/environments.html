Concept Art Process

All 22 environment concept images were generated using Gemini's image generation API. Each location was designed with:

  • Consistent style: Cohesive visual language across all environments
  • Story-driven mood: Lighting and atmosphere that support the narrative
  • Multiple views: Key angles needed for storyboard and 3D reference
  • Color storytelling: Warm tones for home, ethereal for magic, primordial for Jurassic

These concepts guide our Blender 3D environment construction. See the Tech Deep-Dives for details on our AI art pipeline.
