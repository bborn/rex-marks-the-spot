# Color Script - Fairy Dinosaur Date Night

## Overview

This color script defines the emotional color progression throughout "Fairy Dinosaur Date Night." Color is used as a storytelling tool to reinforce the emotional journey of the characters and guide the audience's feelings from scene to scene.

## Emotional Color Journey

### Core Color Themes

| Theme | Colors | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| **Safety/Home** | Warm ambers, golden yellows, cream | Family, security, love |
| **Magic/Supernatural** | Electric blues, purples, magenta | The unknown, portals, fairy magic |
| **Jurassic Wonder** | Lush greens, golden light | Discovery, prehistoric beauty |
| **Danger/Terror** | Dark greens, deep shadows, red accents | Predators, peril, survival |
| **Resolution** | Return to warm palette + rainbow | Reunion, joy, magic integrated into home |

### Color Arc Summary

```
ACT 1: WARM → COOL → ELECTRIC BLUE → LUSH GREEN → DARK
       Home    Storm     Time Warp     Jurassic    T-Rex

ACT 2: GRAY → PURPLE → SUNSET WARM → RED/BLUE
       Investigation  Magic    Emotion     Chase

ACT 3: RAINBOW → EARTHY → BLUE/GOLD → WARM GOLD
       Jetplane   Canyon    Climax      Resolution
```

## Scene-by-Scene Color Breakdown

### ACT 1: THE DEPARTURE & THE JURASSIC

#### Scene 1: Home Evening
- **Palette:** `act1_scene01_home_evening.png`
- **Dominant:** Warm amber (#E8A45C)
- **Function:** Establish safety and love
- **Key:** TV flickers hint at coming disruption; storm visible through windows creates contrast

#### Scene 2: Storm Exterior
- **Palette:** `act1_scene02_storm_exterior.png`
- **Dominant:** Storm blue (#3D5A73)
- **Function:** Transition from safety to danger
- **Key:** Warm house interior visible through door as parents leave

#### Scene 3: Car Night
- **Palette:** `act1_scene03_car_night.png`
- **Dominant:** Night blue with amber dashboard (#2C4A5E / #8B5A2B)
- **Function:** Isolation, tension building
- **Key:** Rain creates visual barrier; couple's argument reflected in cold tones

#### Scene 5: Time Warp (INCITING INCIDENT)
- **Palette:** `act1_scene05_time_warp.png`
- **Dominant:** Electric blue (#00C8FF) and portal purple (#7B2FBE)
- **Function:** Maximum supernatural impact
- **Key:** This is the visual signature of the portal - maintain consistency throughout
- **VFX Note:** Bright white core (#FFFFFF), electrical discharge at edges

#### Scene 6: Aftermath
- **Palette:** `act1_scene06_aftermath.png`
- **Dominant:** Dark room with phone glow (#2D3E50 / #00BFFF)
- **Function:** Shock, uncertainty
- **Key:** Phone glow is the only warm element - it represents connection to parents

#### Scenes 7-8: Jurassic Wonder
- **Palette:** `act1_scene07_jurassic_wonder.png`
- **Dominant:** Lush green (#4A7C59) with golden sun (#FFD700)
- **Function:** Awe, discovery, prehistoric beauty
- **Key:** Most saturated greens of the film; golden light creates sense of wonder

#### Scene 8 (cont.): T-Rex Reveal
- **Palette:** `act1_scene08_trex_terror.png`
- **Dominant:** Dark jungle (#3D5C3D) with danger red (#8B0000)
- **Function:** Terror, survival instinct
- **Key:** Same greens but darker, desaturated; warm highlights only on human faces

#### Scene 10: Cave
- **Palette:** `act1_scene10_cave.png`
- **Dominant:** Cave gray (#3D3D3D) with phone glow (#00BFFF)
- **Function:** Trapped but connected
- **Key:** Phone glow matches portal blue - visual connection between worlds

### ACT 3: THE ADVENTURE & RESOLUTION

#### Jetplane's Colors
- **Palette:** `act3_jetplane_farts.png`
- **Dominant:** Rainbow candy colors against green
- **Function:** Joy, humor, unexpected beauty
- **Key:** Matches Sour Patch Kids colors: Red (#FF6B6B), Yellow (#FFE66D), Teal (#4ECDC4), Purple (#9B59B6), Blue (#3498DB)
- **Production Note:** These are the "signal" colors that guide the parents

#### Canyon Crossing
- **Palette:** `act3_canyon.png`
- **Dominant:** Canyon red-brown (#8B4513) with sky blue (#87CEEB)
- **Function:** Vertigo, peril, teamwork
- **Key:** Crayon bridge colors match the palette - childlike objects in dangerous setting

#### Climax
- **Palette:** `act3_climax.png`
- **Dominant:** Dark storm (#2D3436) with portal blue (#00C8FF) and magic gold (#FFD700)
- **Function:** Maximum stakes
- **Key:** Portal blue and magic gold as the only "hope" colors; T-Rex browns for danger

#### Return Home
- **Palette:** `act3_return.png`
- **Dominant:** Night with warm skin tones
- **Function:** Relief, exhaustion, joy
- **Key:** Police lights now feel protective rather than threatening

#### Epilogue: New Morning
- **Palette:** `epilogue_morning.png`
- **Dominant:** Morning gold (#FFE4B5)
- **Function:** Resolution, new beginning
- **Key:** Return to Act 1 warmth but brighter; rainbow plumes from chimney integrate magic into home

## Key Color Relationships

### Portal Blue (#00C8FF)
The portal/time-warp has a consistent visual signature throughout the film:
- First appears in Scene 5 (inciting incident)
- Echoed in phone glow (Scenes 6, 10, 14-18)
- Returns in climax
- Color represents the connection between time periods

### Warm Amber (#E8A45C)
The "home" color that frames the film:
- Opens the film (Scene 1)
- Appears as dashboard glow in car
- Returns in epilogue
- Represents family, safety, love

### Jetplane Rainbow
The rainbow fart colors serve as:
- Comic relief
- Wayfinding device (parents follow the plumes)
- Visual signature of the movie
- Integration of "magic" into the ordinary world by epilogue

## Usage Guidelines

### For Storyboard Artists
- Reference the palette for each scene when creating panels
- Ensure lighting direction matches emotional intent
- Use warm highlights on character faces to maintain empathy even in dark scenes

### For 3D/Blender Work
- Set up world lighting to match scene palette
- Use color temperature in lights (not just brightness)
- Portal effects should use emission materials with Portal Blue

### For AI Image Generation
Include color references in prompts:
```
"...warm amber lighting, domestic interior, color palette #E8A45C #C67B3C #FFD59E..."
```

### For Final Color Grading
- The color script is a guide, not a rigid specification
- Maintain emotional intent even if specific hex values shift
- Consistency of the portal blue is critical for visual continuity
