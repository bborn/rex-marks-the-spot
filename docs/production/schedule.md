# Production Schedule - Fairy Dinosaur Date Night

**Project:** AI-Generated Animated Movie
**Total Runtime:** ~47.5 minutes
**Format:** 3-Act Structure

---

## Project Scope Summary

### Story Structure

| Act | Scenes | Runtime | Storyboard Panels | Complexity |
|-----|--------|---------|-------------------|------------|
| Act 1 | 1-10 | ~12 min | 65 panels | Moderate (home + Jurassic intro) |
| Act 2 | 11-25 | ~20 min | 89 panels | High (dual storylines) |
| Act 3 | 26-37 | ~15.5 min | 359 panels | Very High (action climax) |
| **Total** | **37 scenes** | **~47.5 min** | **513 panels** | - |

### Key Production Challenges

1. **Dual Timeline Storytelling** - Parallel narratives require visual consistency across time periods
2. **VFX-Heavy Sequences** - Time warp portals, magic spells, dinosaur encounters
3. **Character Consistency** - 6 main characters must maintain appearance across all scenes
4. **Environment Variety** - Modern home, Jurassic jungle, police station, canyon
