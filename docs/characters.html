The Family

Gabe

The Tired Dad

Early 40s, soft around the middle, glasses. A well-meaning workaholic dad whose tinkering hobby leads to the accident that strands him and Nina in the Jurassic era. Resourceful but prone to over-engineering solutions.

Nina

The Elegant Mom

Gabe's wife and the grounding force in the family. Elegant and practical, Nina keeps everyone fed, on schedule, and alive during their prehistoric predicament. Dresses up in a black dress for their date night.

Mia

The Determined Sister (age 8)

The older sibling who takes charge when her parents disappear. Resourceful, brave, and sometimes too quick to act without thinking. Through her adventure, she learns that true leadership means listening and trusting others.

Leo

The Observant Brother (age 5)

Mia's younger brother who sees the world differently. Wears dinosaur pajamas and carries a plush T-Rex toy everywhere. Leo notices patterns and connections others miss, making him essential to solving the biggest challenges they face.
