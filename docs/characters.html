3D Character Models

All six main characters have been modeled in Blender with a stylized, low-poly aesthetic that gives the film its unique visual identity. Each character was designed with these principles:

  • Personality-driven design: Each character's appearance reflects their role and personality
  • Distinctive silhouettes: Every character is recognizable from their outline alone
  • Consistent style: A cohesive low-poly aesthetic across the entire cast
  • Animation-ready: Models structured for efficient rigging and animation
  • Color storytelling: Palettes that reflect each character's emotional journey

The magical characters (Ruben and Jetplane) feature special elements like fairy wings and the dinosaur's unique design. Follow the blog for updates on animation progress.
