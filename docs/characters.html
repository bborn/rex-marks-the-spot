Character Design Process

We're still in the early stages of character design. Our process involves:

  • Personality first: Understanding who each character is before deciding how they look
  • Silhouette testing: Ensuring each character is recognizable from their outline alone
  • Expression sheets: Exploring the range of emotions each character needs to convey
  • Color studies: Finding palettes that reflect personality and work in various lighting
  • 3D modeling: Translating 2D concepts into Blender-ready assets

As designs are approved, we'll share concept art, development sketches, and final 3D models here. Follow the blog for updates.
