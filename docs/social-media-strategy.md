# Rex Marks The Spot - Social Media Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the social media presence and content strategy for "Rex Marks The Spot" / "Fairy Dinosaur Date Night" - an open-source AI-assisted filmmaking project.

## Brand Voice

**Tone**: Enthusiastic but honest, technically curious, creative, transparent
**Personality**: The excited filmmaker sharing their journey - wins AND failures
**Unique angle**: We're making a movie with AI tools and showing everything

### Key Messages
- This is an experiment in AI-assisted filmmaking
- We share the real process - not just polished results
- AI is a tool, not a replacement for creativity
- Open source, open process, open to feedback

### YouTube (@RexMarksTheSpot)

**Purpose**: Long-form content - trailers, devlogs, tutorials, behind-the-scenes
**Frequency**: 1-2 videos per month (quality over quantity)
**Content Types**:
- Progress reels / Monthly recap videos
- Technical deep-dives (Blender + AI workflow)
- Character reveal videos
- Eventually: Trailers, final film

**Channel Description**:

Rex Marks The Spot is an experiment in AI-assisted filmmaking.
