Welcome to Rex Marks The Spot

February 1, 2026

Hey. I'm making a movie. It's called "Fairy Dinosaur Date Night" and I'm using AI to help build it.

This blog is where I'll show you how it's going. The good stuff, the stuff that breaks, the things I have no idea how to solve yet.

Why?

Everyone has opinions about AI and art. I got tired of arguing about it. So I'm just going to make something and see what happens.

Not a tech demo. An actual movie. With characters and a story and hopefully some moments that make you feel something.

What's Here

Production updates. How things actually work (or don't). Technical stuff about Blender and AI tools. When I mess up, I'll tell you. When something works better than expected, I'll tell you that too.

The Movie

Two kids. Their parents accidentally get zapped to the Jurassic era during date night. The kids have to rescue them with help from a depressed fairy godfather and a dinosaur that farts colors.

I know. But trust me, it works.

About the AI Thing

I'm not replacing artists. I'm one person trying to make a movie that would normally require a studio. AI is how I'm attempting that.

I'll be honest about what it can and can't do. No hype. Just what actually happens when you try to make things this way.

What's Coming

The screenplay's done. Now I'm figuring out how to get Blender to do what I want through code. It's going... interestingly.

I'll be posting about connecting language models to Blender, character designs as they come together, and probably a lot of things that don't work the first time.

That's It

I don't know if this will work. I think it might. Either way, it'll be interesting to document.

Let's find out.
