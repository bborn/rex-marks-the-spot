## Instagram

**Caption:**

From $50 per scene to $0.25 per scene. That's not a sale - that's a different model.

Last week we accidentally burned through $50 generating video with Google Veo in a single afternoon. This week we discovered P-Video (prunaai/p-video on Replicate) and everything changed.

The numbers: Veo costs $0.40 per second of generated video. P-Video costs $0.005. That's 80x cheaper.

We rebuilt our entire Scene 1 animatic - 9 clips, ~45 seconds of footage - for roughly a quarter. Then we pushed into Scene 3 for the first time: 4 new clips assembled into an animatic with crossfade transitions.

The quality is noticeably lower than Veo. Softer details, less precise motion. But for drafting and figuring out timing and story flow? More than enough. And when you're not afraid to hit "generate," you try more things and get to better results faster.

We also built a model-agnostic video generation layer so we can swap between Veo and P-Video with a single line of code. Draft cheap, then selectively upgrade the shots that matter.

Ten days ago, generating video felt like burning money. Now it feels like sketching.
