About This Project

Fairy Dinosaur Date Night is an animated film we're creating as an experiment in AI-assisted filmmaking. We're documenting everything ‚Äî the wins, the failures, and the weird middle ground where AI helps but doesn't replace human creativity.

The story follows Mia and Leo, two siblings whose parents accidentally get transported to the Jurassic era during a date night gone wrong. Their only hope? A depressed fairy godfather named Ruben and his magical companion Jetplane ‚Äî a dinosaur with a very unusual talent.

Our Approach

  • Transparent process: We share our actual workflow, including what doesn't work
  • AI as a tool: Using LLMs and automation to assist, not replace, creative decisions
  • Open documentation: Technical deep-dives on our Blender pipeline and tools
  • Community-first: Inviting feedback and discussion as we build
