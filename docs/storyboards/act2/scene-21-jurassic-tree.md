# Scene 21: EXT. JURASSIC - NIGHT

**Duration:** 2:30 | **Visual Complexity:** 4 | **Panels:** 8

## Setting
High in a massive Jurassic tree. Night. Parents have climbed for safety. Stars visible through prehistoric canopy. Most intimate and emotional scene of Act 2.

## Characters
- **Nina** (Mother): Vulnerable, emotional, confessing fears
- **Gabe** (Father): Supportive, also vulnerable, making promises

## VFX Requirements
- **Environment:** Massive tree, Jurassic nightscape
- **Phone effects:** Battery countdown visible

## Panel 21C: Two-Shot - Settling on Branch
**Shot Type:** Two-shot | **Duration:** 20s | **Camera:** STATIC

### Composition
Parents settling into position for the night. Finding comfortable spots on the wide branch.

### Character Positions
- Nina: Screen left, leaning against trunk
- Gabe: Screen right, near her
- Branch: Wide enough to sit safely
- Trunk: Support behind them

### Key Action
Physical settling leads to emotional settling. They're safe for now.

### Visual Details
- Branch wide enough for two
- Tree trunk at their backs
- Gabe's tuxedo jacket placed as cushion for Nina
- Both exhausted
- Intimate physical proximity
- Night sky visible through gaps in canopy

## Panel 21D: Close-up - Emotional Talk
**Shot Type:** Close-up Nina | **Duration:** 40s | **Camera:** SLOW PUSH

### Composition
Nina's face in profile, then turning to camera. The confession begins.

### Character Positions
- Nina: Center, emotional

### Key Action
Nina's confession. She hates the galas. She misses family time. This changes everything.

### Camera Movement
Very slow push toward Nina's face.

### Visual Details
- Makeup completely gone/smeared
- Hair wild, undone
- Vulnerable expression
- Eyes glistening
- Moonlight on face
- Raw honesty visible

### Dialogue
**NINA:** "Gabe... I need to tell you something."
**NINA:** "I hate those galas."
**GABE (O.S.):** "What?"
**NINA:** "I hate them. I've always hated them. The dresses, the small talk, the... the pretending to care about things that don't matter."
**NINA:** "I'd rather be home with the kids. Watching dinosaur movies. Eating pizza."
**NINA:** "What if... what if I never get to do that again?"

### Sound Design
Emotional silence, night sounds, Nina's voice catching
