## Intercut Structure (Scenes 14-18)

The Connection sequence requires careful intercutting:

```
14 CAVE - Nina discovers connection (1:00)
    ↓
15 CAR - Kids hear static (0:15)
    ↓
16 CAVE - Nina calls louder (0:30)
    ↓
17 CAR - Kids scream, can't be heard (0:20)
    ↓
18 CAVE TUNNEL - Signal fades (0:30)
```

**Edit Pattern:** Quick cuts when voices overlap. Longer holds on emotional reactions. Phone audio serves as audio bridge between locations.
