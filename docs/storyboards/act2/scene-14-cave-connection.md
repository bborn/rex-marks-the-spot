# Scene 14: INT. JURASSIC CAVE - DAY

**Duration:** 1:00 | **Visual Complexity:** 3 | **Panels:** 5

## Setting
Inside the Jurassic cave from Act 1. T-Rex is outside dismembering the second dinosaur (off-screen sounds). Nina and Gabe recovering, inspecting the phone.

## Characters
- **Nina** (Mother): Disheveled gala dress, desperate hope
- **Gabe** (Father): Torn tuxedo, exhausted but thinking

## VFX Requirements
- **Phone effects:** Static screen, frozen display, occasional crackle
- **Battery indicator:** 65% visible
