# Scene 20: INT. POLICE STATION - NIGHT

**Duration:** 4:00 | **Visual Complexity:** 2 | **Panels:** 10

## Setting
Police station conference room converted to makeshift sleeping area for the kids. Night. Ruben the "janitor" appears and reveals himself as their Fairy Godfather.

## Characters
- **Mia** (~8): Suspicious at first, then hopeful
- **Leo** (~5): Excited, can't keep secrets
- **Ruben** (Fairy Godfather): Disguised as janitor, droopy wings, tired eyes
- **Detective McNattin**: Brief appearances, unaware

## VFX Requirements
- **Ruben's wings:** Droopy iridescent wings (when revealed)
- **Magic demonstrations:** Mop-wand effects (sparkles, minor transformations)
