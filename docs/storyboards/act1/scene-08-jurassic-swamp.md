# Scene 8: EXT. JURASSIC SWAMP - DAY

**Duration:** 2:00 | **Visual Complexity:** 5 (MAJOR VFX) | **Panels:** 9

## Setting
Jurassic era swamp exterior. Day. Lush prehistoric vegetation. This is the MAJOR DINOSAUR REVEAL scene - T-Rex appears and destroys the car.

## Characters
- **Nina** (Mother): Marveling, then terrified
- **Gabe** (Father): Skeptical, then terrified
- **Weird Creature** (Future Jetplane): Cute, investigating time warp

## VFX Requirements
- **T-REX:** Full CG or animatronic combo (major creature reveal)
- **Time Warp (nearly closed):** Small blue vortex
- **Weird Creature (Jetplane):** Puppet + CG enhancement
- **Environment:** Extensive CG/matte painting augmentation
- **Car destruction:** Practical + VFX

---

## Panel 8A: Wide - Parents Emerge
**Shot Type:** Wide establishing | **Duration:** 15s | **Camera:** CRANE DOWN to ground level

### Composition
Parents stepping out of crashed car into the swamp. Full environment reveal. Sky, vegetation, atmosphere all visible.

### Character Positions
- Nina: Exiting passenger side
- Gabe: Struggling out of jammed driver door (goes through passenger side)
- Car: Center, clearly damaged
- Environment: Surrounding, overwhelming

### Key Action
Nina steps out first, awestruck. Gabe follows, mid-sentence, stops when he sees surroundings.

### Camera Movement
Start high (showing scale of environment), crane down to ground level with characters.

### Visual Details
- Crashed car looks small against massive plants
- Steam/smoke from car hood
- Nina in disheveled formal wear
- Gabe in rumpled tuxedo
- Prehistoric plants dwarf them
- In background (unnoticed): small time warp and weird creature

### Dialogue
**GABE:** "...we probably need to call Jenny too, she's gonna be wondering what... the... heck is going on...??"

### Sound Design
Full jungle ambiance, car clicking/cooling

## Panel 8F: Wide - T-REX APPEARS
**Shot Type:** Wide | **Duration:** 20s | **Camera:** STATIC then slow TILT UP

### Composition
THE REVEAL. Massive T-REX emerges through the trees. This is THE hero shot of the first dinosaur encounter.

### Character Positions
- T-Rex: Entering from screen right through trees
- Nina & Gabe: Small in frame left, backs to us
- Car: Between them and T-Rex

### Key Action
1. Trees PART (5s)
2. T-Rex head emerges (5s)
3. Full body visible, investigating (10s)

### Camera Movement
Starts at character height, slow tilt up to show full T-Rex scale.

### Visual Details
**T-REX:**
- Massive scale (contrast to tiny humans)
- Realistic coloring (earth tones, not cartoonish)
- Predatory intelligence in eyes
- Head tilting, investigating
- 40+ feet tall
- Teeth visible

**ENVIRONMENT:**
- Trees/ferns parted/crushed by T-Rex
- Dramatic lighting (sun behind T-Rex = silhouette effect)
- Steam/mist adds atmosphere

### Dialogue
**GABE:** "We need to... run!"

### Sound Design
Trees cracking, ROAR (building), car alarm triggers from vibration
