# Scene 3: INT. CAR - NIGHT

**Duration:** 0:45 | **Visual Complexity:** 2 | **Panels:** 4

## Setting
Interior of Gabe's car. Night. Rain pounding on roof and windshield. Wipers working overtime. Occasional lightning illuminates interior.

## Characters
- **Gabe** (Father): Driving, tense, focused on road
- **Nina** (Mother): Passenger seat, searching for phone, then having important conversation
