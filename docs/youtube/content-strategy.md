# YouTube Content Strategy

## Channel

- **Name:** Rex Marks the Spot
- **URL:** https://www.youtube.com/@rex_the_movie
- **Focus:** Behind-the-scenes of an AI-produced animated movie

## Posting Schedule

- **Frequency:** 1-2 videos per week
- **Best days:** Tuesday and Thursday (typical YouTube engagement peaks)

## Content Types

### Character Showcases
- Introduce each character (Gabe, Nina, Mia, Leo, Ruben, Jetplane)
- Show concept art to 3D model pipeline
- Before/after comparisons

### 3D Process Videos
- AI model generation with Tripo, Meshy, Hyper3D Rodin
- Blender scene assembly and lighting
- Texture and material workflows

### Storyboard Reveals
- Walk through scenes panel by panel
- Compare storyboard sketches to rendered output
- Story and script development insights

### Production Updates
- Monthly progress recaps
- Tool reviews and workflow tips
- Milestone celebrations (first render, first animation, etc.)

## SEO Keywords

Primary: AI animation, indie film, animated movie, AI filmmaking
Secondary: AI movie making, Blender animation, AI generated movie, indie animated film
Long-tail: how to make an animated movie with AI, AI tools for filmmaking, making a movie with artificial intelligence

## Thumbnail Style

- Bold text overlay
- Character art or 3D renders
- High contrast, bright colors
- Consistent branding (Rex Marks the Spot logo)

## Video Format

- **Short form (under 60s):** Quick process clips, character reveals
- **Long form (5-15 min):** Deep dives into workflow, scene breakdowns
- **Target:** Mix of both to feed Shorts algorithm and build watch time
