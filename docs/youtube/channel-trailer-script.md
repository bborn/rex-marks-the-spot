# YouTube Channel Trailer Script

**Duration:** 45 seconds | **Resolution:** 1920x1080 | **FPS:** 30

---

## Scene Breakdown

### HOOK (0s - 4s)
**Visual:** Family portrait, slow zoom. Crossfade to storyboard panel of the time warp crash.
**Text:** "What if date night..." -> "...went VERY wrong?"
**Audio:** Warm music begins, then dramatic beat on "wrong"

### TITLE CARD (4s - 8s)
**Visual:** Title logo on dark purple background with golden glow pulse.
**Text:** "Fairy Dinosaur Date Night" / "An AI-Animated Movie"
**Audio:** Music swells, magical shimmer SFX

### MEET THE CAST (8s - 12.5s)
**Visual:** Character turnarounds pop in one by one (Gabe, Nina, Mia, Leo).
**Text:** "Meet the Family" + character name labels
**Audio:** Upbeat, playful theme

### THE STORY (12.5s - 18.5s)
**Visual:** Storyboard montage - quick cuts through Act 1 panels (home, driving, storm).
**Text:** "A simple date night..."
**Audio:** Building tension, storyboard pencil-scratch SFX

### THE TWIST (18.5s - 23.5s)
**Visual:** Storyboard montage - adventure panels (time warp, T-Rex, cave).
**Text:** "...becomes a Jurassic adventure"
**Audio:** Big orchestral hit, then adventure theme

### 3D SHOWCASE (23.5s - 29.5s)
**Visual:** 3D model renders cycle through (Gabe, Nina, Mia, Leo textured models).
**Text:** "From 2D to 3D" + model labels
**Audio:** Techy/futuristic underlay

### AI PIPELINE (29.5s - 34.5s)
**Visual:** Dark gradient with animated text about AI tools used.
**Text:** "Built with AI" / "Gemini | Remotion | Blender | Meshy" / "Every frame. Every model. Every scene."
**Audio:** Electronic pulse, building energy

### SECRET CHARACTERS (34.5s - 39.5s)
**Visual:** Ruben and Jetplane character reveals.
**Text:** "And some unexpected friends..." + character labels
**Audio:** Comedic/whimsical flourish

### CTA (39.5s - 45s)
**Visual:** Dark background, large text with glowing subscribe button.
**Text:** "Follow the Journey" / "Made entirely with AI" / "SUBSCRIBE" / "rexmarksthespot.com"
**Audio:** Music resolves, final hit

## Tone
- Playful and warm, like a Pixar "making of" teaser
- Emphasis on the novelty of AI-produced animation
- Family-friendly, slightly whimsical
- Not taking itself too seriously

## Key Messages
1. This is a real animated movie being made with AI
2. The characters and story are charming and fun
3. The production pipeline is technically impressive
4. Viewers should subscribe to follow the journey
