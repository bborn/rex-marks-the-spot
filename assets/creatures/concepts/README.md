# Dinosaur Species Concept Art

Concept art for additional dinosaur species in "Fairy Dinosaur Date Night". All designs follow the Pixar/Dreamworks animation style established in the storyboards.

## Species Overview

### Friendly Herbivore Herd
Background dinosaurs that populate the Jurassic world, showing it's not all predators.

| Image | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `gentle_brachiosaurus_family_v1.png` | Family of gentle Brachiosaurus - parent pair with two babies. Sage green with cream bellies, warm expressions. |
| `playful_triceratops_group_v1.png` | Playful Triceratops group with rounded horns. Terracotta orange and cream, spotted patterns. |
| `peaceful_parasaurolophus_herd_v1.png` | Elegant Parasaurolophus herd at a misty lakeside. Blue-purple gradient with subtle bioluminescence. |
