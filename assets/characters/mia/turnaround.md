# Mia Bornsztein - Character Turnaround

## Overview
**Role**: Kid Protagonist (Older Sister)
**Age**: Approximately 8 years old
**Personality**: Responsible, determined, protective of her younger brother, rises to challenges

## Physical Description

### Body Proportions
- **Height**: 4'2" (slightly tall for her age, emphasizes her "older sibling" role)
- **Build**: Slim, athletic frame suggesting an active child
- **Head-to-body ratio**: 1:4.5 (stylized for animation, slightly larger head for expressiveness)
- **Silhouette**: Distinctive ponytail creates recognizable profile

### Facial Features
- **Face shape**: Oval with soft cheekbones
- **Eyes**: Large, expressive brown eyes with visible catchlights; slightly upturned corners suggesting optimism
- **Eyebrows**: Defined but soft; very expressive for emotional range
- **Nose**: Small, slightly upturned button nose
- **Mouth**: Expressive lips, natural rose tint; easy smile but can show determination
- **Ears**: Average size, usually partially covered by hair

### Hair
- **Style**: Mid-length brown hair usually worn in a high ponytail with loose strands framing face
- **Color**: Warm chestnut brown with subtle highlights
- **Texture**: Slightly wavy, gives movement during action scenes
- **Signature element**: Bright turquoise scrunchie/hair tie (connects to family color palette)

### Costume Design

#### Primary Outfit (Adventure)
- **Top**: Purple t-shirt with small star graphic, comfortable and practical
- **Bottoms**: Denim shorts with cuffed hem
- **Footwear**: Pink/white sneakers with velcro straps
- **Accessories**: Small backpack (appears later in adventure)

#### Opening Scene Outfit
- **Pajamas**: Light blue PJs with cloud pattern (establishes domestic setting)

## Key Views for 3D Modeling

### Front View
- Symmetrical stance, weight evenly distributed
- Arms at sides, palms facing thighs
- Ponytail visible behind head
- Clear view of facial features and expression

### Side View (Profile)
- Ponytail extends back and slightly up
- Natural slight S-curve to spine
- Nose profile defined
- Ear placement visible

### Back View
- Ponytail detail important (hair tie, flow of hair)
- Shirt design visible from behind
- Posture suggests confidence

### 3/4 View
- Most flattering angle for marketing materials
- Shows dimension of facial features
- Dynamic potential for poses

## Rigging Notes
- **Facial rig**: Needs extensive range for worry, determination, fear, joy, relief
- **Hair physics**: Ponytail should have secondary motion, bounce during movement
- **Body rig**: Athletic poses needed - running, climbing, reaching
- **Hand rig**: Detailed fingers for grabbing, pointing, holding objects

## Scale Reference
- Compared to Leo: Mia is approximately 6 inches taller
- Compared to parents: Reaches about hip/waist height on adults
- Compared to Jetplane (small form): About same height
