## Notes for Generation
- Hair scrunchie should ALWAYS be turquoise - key recognition element
- Purple shirt is primary identifier
- Eyes should be large and expressive (animation style)
- Maintain warm, approachable feeling throughout
- Reference: Think young Pixar heroine (Brave, Inside Out style)
