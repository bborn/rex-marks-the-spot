## Emotional Color Notes

### Lighting Adjustments
- **Happy scenes**: Warm golden overlay (+10% yellow)
- **Scary scenes**: Cool blue overlay (+15% blue, -10% saturation)
- **Jurassic scenes**: Green ambient bounce from foliage
- **Night scenes**: Desaturate 20%, increase purple in shadows

### Character Consistency
- Hair scrunchie ALWAYS turquoise - recognition element
- Purple shirt is primary identifier - maintains in all lighting
- Skin tone warms in emotional moments, cools in danger

## Technical Notes

### For 3D Rendering
- Subsurface scattering for skin: 0.3-0.4 strength
- Hair uses gradient shader with root-to-tip variation
- Fabric materials need appropriate roughness (cotton: 0.7-0.8)
- Eyes need high-gloss clearcoat for life

### For 2D Concept Art
- Use multiply layers for shadows
- Screen/overlay for highlights
- Keep saturation consistent across all art
