# Mia Bornsztein - Key Expressions

## Overview
Mia is the emotional anchor for the children's storyline. Her expressions need to convey both vulnerability and strength, showing a child who is scared but brave.

---

## Primary Expressions

### 1. Determined/Brave
**Scene Usage**: Taking charge of the rescue mission, confronting obstacles
**Key Features**:
- Eyebrows slightly furrowed in center
- Jaw set, mouth closed in firm line
- Eyes focused with slight squint
- Chin slightly raised
- Nostrils may flare subtly

**Reference Scenes**: "We have to save them ourselves" moments

---

### 2. Worried/Concerned
**Scene Usage**: When parents are missing, when Leo is in danger
**Key Features**:
- Inner eyebrows raised (worry lines)
- Eyes wide but with contracted pupils
- Lower lip slightly bitten or tucked
- Hands often accompany this expression (wringing, touching face)

**Reference Scenes**: Discovering parents' car disappeared, hearing about time warp

---

### 3. Loving/Protective (Big Sister)
**Scene Usage**: Comforting Leo, family reunions
**Key Features**:
- Soft, warm eyes with slight lid droop
- Gentle smile, slight head tilt
- Eyebrows relaxed, slightly raised
- Often paired with physical touch (hand on Leo's shoulder)

**Reference Scenes**: Promising Leo they'll find mom and dad

---

### 4. Fear/Startled
**Scene Usage**: Dinosaur encounters, dangerous situations
**Key Features**:
- Eyes wide open (white visible around iris)
- Eyebrows high and curved
- Mouth open, corners pulled back
- Body often accompanies with tension/recoil

**Reference Scenes**: First T-Rex encounter, portal travel

---

### 5. Joy/Relief
**Scene Usage**: Finding parents, successful rescues, ending
**Key Features**:
- Big genuine smile (teeth showing)
- Eyes crinkled at corners (Duchenne smile)
- Eyebrows raised slightly
- Whole face lifts upward
- Often involves laughter or tears of joy

**Reference Scenes**: Family reunion, returning home safely

---

### 6. Frustrated/Exasperated
**Scene Usage**: When things go wrong, dealing with Ruben's failed magic
**Key Features**:
- Eyes roll or look upward
- Exhale (cheeks puff slightly)
- Hands may go to hips
- One eyebrow raised higher than other

**Reference Scenes**: Ruben's magic misfires, obstacles blocking progress

---

### 7. Amazed/Wonder
**Scene Usage**: Seeing Jurassic world, magical moments
**Key Features**:
- Eyes wide with dilated pupils
- Mouth slightly open (jaw dropped)
- Eyebrows raised
- Head may tilt back slightly

**Reference Scenes**: First view of Jurassic landscape, meeting Jetplane

---

## Secondary Expressions

### 8. Skeptical/Doubtful
- One eyebrow raised
- Lips pressed together, slightly to one side
- Head tilted
- Arms may cross

### 9. Thinking/Problem-Solving
- Eyes looking up and to the side
- Finger to chin or lip
- Slight frown of concentration
- Eyebrows drawn together but not angry

### 10. Sneaky/Conspiratorial
- Small smile with closed lips
- Eyes narrowed playfully
- Leaning in posture
- Used when planning with Leo

---

## Expression Transitions

### Fear → Determination
Key moment in character arc. Fear expression should be able to morph into determination with:
1. Eyes narrowing from wide
2. Jaw setting from slack
3. Eyebrows moving from raised to furrowed

### Worried → Relief
Used multiple times in story:
1. Tension releases from forehead
2. Held breath releases (shoulders drop)
3. Eyes soften, tears may form
4. Smile slowly emerges

---

## Mouth Shapes (Phonemes)
For dialogue, Mia needs full phoneme set:
- A/I - open mouth
- O/U - rounded lips
- E - wide smile shape
- M/B/P - closed lips
- F/V - lower lip under teeth
- TH - tongue visible
- L - tongue up

---

## Notes for Animators
- Mia's default resting expression is alert but approachable
- She processes emotions visibly - audience should read her feelings easily
- Transitions between expressions should feel natural, not snappy
- When scared, she fights to control her expression (conflict visible)
- Her brave moments are earned, not effortless
