# Jenny - Character Turnaround

## Overview
**Role**: Babysitter
**Age**: ~15 years old (teenager)
**Personality**: Cheerful, somewhat oblivious, phone-obsessed; nervous but earnest when questioned
**Arc**: Background character who unknowingly implicates Gabe through innocent testimony

## Physical Description

### Body Proportions
- **Height**: 5'4" (average teen height)
- **Build**: Slim, typical teenager; slightly lanky
- **Head-to-body ratio**: 1:6.5 (youthful proportions, slightly stylized)
- **Silhouette**: Ponytail and phone-in-hand create recognizable profile

### Facial Features
- **Face shape**: Round with soft features, still has some baby-face quality
- **Eyes**: Blue, wide, often looking at phone screen; expressive when anxious
- **Eyebrows**: Light, natural, not heavily shaped (she's 15)
- **Nose**: Small, slightly upturned
- **Mouth**: Usually in a casual half-smile; bites lip when nervous
- **Skin**: Fair with light freckles across nose and cheeks
- **Defining feature**: Perpetual phone glow on face

### Hair
- **Style**: Blonde hair pulled back in a ponytail, slightly messy/casual
- **Color**: Bright blonde, natural-looking
- **Texture**: Straight to slightly wavy, some wisps around face
- **Ponytail**: Mid-height, practical teen style (not high fashion)

### Costume Design

#### Primary Outfit (Babysitting Night)
- **Top**: Oversized pastel hoodie (light pink or lavender), sleeves slightly pushed up
- **Bottoms**: Leggings or skinny jeans, dark colored
- **Footwear**: White sneakers or slip-on Vans
- **Accessories**: Phone (always), small stud earrings, simple friendship bracelet
- **Backpack**: Canvas tote bag (brought with her, set down somewhere)

#### Morning After (Scene 12 - Interview)
- **Same outfit** as babysitting night (she stayed over)
- Slightly rumpled, tired-looking
- Hoodie pulled tighter, more self-conscious

- **Facial rig**: Cheerful obliviousness, nervousness, wide-eyed surprise, lip-biting anxiety
- **Hair physics**: Simple ponytail sway, less complex than Mia's
- **Body rig**: Casual teen movements, texting posture, fidgeting in interview
- **Hand rig**: Phone-holding poses essential, fidgeting gestures

- Often looking down at phone, distracted
- Startled reaction when TV explodes (Scene 6)
