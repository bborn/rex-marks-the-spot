## Character Arc Colors

### Act 1 (Depressed)
- Overall palette: Muted, gray-shifted
- Wings: Dull, droopy
- Skin: Sallow, tired
- Clothes: Maximum dishevelment

### Act 2 (Reluctantly Engaged)
- Palette begins warming slightly
- Wings: Occasional shimmer
- Skin: Slight improvement
- Clothes: Same but less noticed

### Act 3 (Heroic)
- Full color returns
- Wings: Brilliant, glowing
- Skin: Healthy undertones emerge
- Clothes: Still worn, but worn with PURPOSE

## Technical Notes

### Wing Rendering
- Subsurface scattering for membrane
- Iridescence shader for color shift
- Particle system for magic dust
- Transparency varies with emotion

### Skin Complexity
- Under-eye circles: separate layer
- Stubble: texture map with slight displacement
- Can lighten/brighten through film

### Fabric
- Wrinkle normals important
- Moth holes: alpha cutout
- Stains: decal system for variation

### Magic Effects
- Particle systems
- Glow/bloom in post
- Color keyed to success/failure
