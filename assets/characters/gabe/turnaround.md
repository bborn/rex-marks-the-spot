# Gabe Bornsztein - Character Turnaround

## Overview
**Role**: Father
**Age**: Late 30s (38)
**Personality**: Workaholic, well-meaning but distracted, learns to prioritize family
**Arc**: Starts prioritizing work over family, ends valuing presence over productivity

## Physical Description

### Body Proportions
- **Height**: 6'0" (tall, authoritative presence)
- **Build**: Average to slightly soft - desk job physique with hints of former athleticism
- **Head-to-body ratio**: 1:7 (realistic adult proportions, slightly stylized)
- **Silhouette**: Broad shoulders, slightly rounded midsection, distinctive profile with glasses

### Facial Features
- **Face shape**: Oval tending toward square jaw
- **Eyes**: Brown, warm but often distracted; wears rectangular glasses
- **Eyebrows**: Thick, expressive, show stress easily
- **Nose**: Medium, straight with slight bump (character)
- **Mouth**: Full lips, natural resting expression is slightly concerned
- **Facial hair**: Five o'clock shadow (perpetually slightly unshaven from overwork)
- **Ears**: Average, partially visible
- **Defining feature**: Rectangular glasses with thin dark frames

### Hair
- **Style**: Short, professional cut with slight natural wave
- **Color**: Dark brown with early gray at temples (stress indicator)
- **Texture**: Thick but managed
- **Note**: Gets increasingly disheveled in Jurassic scenes

### Costume Design

- **Glasses**: Need to move with face, catch light
- **Body rig**: Professional poses but also running, climbing, protective stances
- **Hand rig**: Phone holding, reaching for kids, adjusting glasses (nervous habit)

## Animation Guidelines
- Starts with distracted, always-moving energy (checking watch, phone)
- Gradually becomes more present and focused on family

- Running is slightly awkward in dress shoes
- Glasses adjustment is nervous tic
