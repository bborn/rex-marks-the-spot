## Costume Progression

### Stage 1: Opening (Pristine)
- White shirt: #FFFFFF
- Tie: perfectly knotted
- Pants: pressed, clean
- Shoes: polished

### Stage 2: Crash Landing
- Shirt: 15% dirt overlay
- Tie: loosened
- Pants: dusty
- Glasses: slightly askew

### Stage 3: Mid-Jurassic
- Shirt: 40% dirt, sleeves rolled
- Tie: removed or around head as bandana
- Pants: torn at knee
- Hair: disheveled

### Stage 4: Rescue/Return
- Shirt: 60% dirty but "battle-worn hero"
- Full "dad who went through hell" look
- But face is relaxed, present

## Technical Notes

### For 3D Rendering
- Glasses need refraction and reflection
- Stubble via texture/displacement
- Fabric wrinkle progression over scenes
- Dirt accumulation shader

### Glasses Rendering
- IOR: 1.5 for lens
- Anti-reflective coating effect
- Frame: slight metallic roughness
- Clean in opening, smudged later
