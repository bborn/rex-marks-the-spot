## Costume Progression

### Stage 1: Gala Ready
- Dress pristine
- Hair perfect updo
- Makeup flawless
- Heels on

### Stage 2: Crash
- Dress slightly mussed
- Hair starting to fall
- Makeup intact but stressed
- One heel broken?

### Stage 3: Jurassic Survival
- Dress torn at hem (mobility)
- Hair down or practical ponytail
- Makeup wearing off
- Barefoot

### Stage 4: End
- Dress battle-worn but dignified
- Hair natural and free
- Natural beauty
- "I survived dinosaurs" energy

## Technical Notes

### For 3D Rendering
- Hair shader needs high gloss
- Fabric needs proper drape simulation
- Gold jewelry: metallic shader with appropriate roughness
- Eye shader needs complex iris rendering

### Dress Material
- Start: Silk-like sheen
- Progress: More matte as dirty
- Tears: Fray realistically

### For 2D Concept Art
- Emphasize elegance in early designs
- Show transformation through ordeal
- Natural beauty should emerge, not diminish
- Strength grows as polish fades
