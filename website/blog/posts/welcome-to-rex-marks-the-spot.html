Why We're Doing This

AI in creative work is a controversial topic. Some see it as a threat to artists. Others see it as a revolutionary tool. We think the truth is more nuanced, and the only way to understand it is to actually build something.

So that's what we're doing. We're making an animated short film â€” not a tech demo, but an actual story with characters you'll care about. And we're using AI to help us do it faster, differently, and maybe even better than we could alone.

The Story

"Fairy Dinosaur Date Night" follows two siblings, Mia and Leo, whose parents accidentally get transported to the Jurassic era during what was supposed to be a simple date night. Their only hope for rescue is a depressed fairy godfather named Ruben and his companion Jetplane â€” a dinosaur with a very unusual (and colorful) talent.

Our Approach to AI

Let's be clear about something: we're not trying to replace artists. We're trying to understand how AI can assist the creative process without losing what makes art meaningful.

Here's our philosophy:

  • AI as a tool, not a replacement: Every creative decision still goes through human judgment
  • Transparency over hype: We'll show you what AI can actually do, not what marketing claims
  • Honest about limitations: When something doesn't work, we'll tell you
  • Open process: Our research and many of our tools will be shared publicly
