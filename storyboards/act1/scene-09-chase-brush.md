# Scene 9: EXT. JURASSIC DENSE BRUSH - DAY

**Duration:** 1:00 | **Visual Complexity:** 5 (ACTION VFX) | **Panels:** 7

## Setting
Dense Jurassic forest/jungle. Day. Heavy vegetation. Chase sequence. T-Rex and second dinosaur pursuing parents.

## Characters
- **Nina** (Mother): Running, screaming, arguing
- **Gabe** (Father): Running, arguing, spotted escape routes
- **T-Rex:** Primary pursuer
- **Second Dinosaur:** New threat that blocks their path

## VFX Requirements
- **T-Rex chase:** Full CG, destruction effects
- **Second Dinosaur:** Full CG (different species)
- **Environment destruction:** Trees/ferns being crushed
- **Dense vegetation:** Heavy CG enhancement

## Panel 9A: Steadicam - Chase Through Ferns
**Shot Type:** Steadicam chase | **Duration:** 15s | **Camera:** STEADICAM following behind characters

### Composition
Following Nina and Gabe as they crash through massive prehistoric ferns. T-Rex visible in background, gaining.

### Character Positions
- Nina: Screen right, slightly ahead
- Gabe: Screen left, keeping pace
- T-Rex: Background center, crashing through

### Key Action
Full sprint through dense vegetation. Formal wear getting shredded. Pure survival.

### Camera Movement
Steadicam follows behind and slightly to side, showing both characters and the threat behind.

### Visual Details
- Giant ferns (human height+) being pushed aside
- Ground uneven, puddles splashing
- Nina's dress torn
- Gabe's tux jacket abandoned/torn
- T-Rex demolishing everything in path
- Dust/debris flying

### Dialogue
**GABE:** "Go go go go!"

### Sound Design
Crashing vegetation, heavy breathing, T-Rex footsteps, roar
