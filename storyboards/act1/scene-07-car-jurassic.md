# Scene 7: INT. GABE'S CAR - DAY

**Duration:** 1:30 | **Visual Complexity:** 3 | **Panels:** 6

## Setting
Interior of crashed car in Jurassic swamp. Day. Car interior is trashed from the trip through the portal - debris, toys, water dripping, smoke from hood.

## Characters
- **Nina** (Mother): Waking up, confused, then awed
- **Gabe** (Father): Unconscious, then groggy

---

## Panel 7A: Close-up - Nina Wakes
**Shot Type:** Close-up | **Duration:** 15s | **Camera:** STATIC, soft focus to sharp

### Composition
Nina's face, eyes closed, then slowly opening. Disoriented. Confusion. Light different than before (daylight, not night).

### Character Positions
- Nina: Filling frame
- Phone: Visible in her hand

### Key Action
Nina's eyes flutter open. Slow breathing. Looking around without moving head. Processing.

### Visual Details
- Soft, warm light on face (not storm light)
- Dirt/debris on cheek
- Hair disheveled
- Eyes adjusting to light
- Confusion in expression

### Dialogue
*(No dialogue - waking)*

### Sound Design
Distant exotic birds, water dripping, subtle engine ticking

---

## Panel 7B: POV - Trashed Car Interior
**Shot Type:** POV from Nina's position | **Duration:** 10s | **Camera:** SLOW PAN

### Composition
Nina's POV scanning car interior. Chaos. Debris everywhere. Daylight streaming through cracked windshield.

### Character Positions
- Gabe: Visible slumped against window (seen from Nina's POV)
- Debris: Scattered throughout

### Key Action
POV scans across dashboard, debris, sees Gabe unconscious.

### Camera Movement
Slow pan left to right mimicking Nina looking around.

### Visual Details
- Random items scattered: papers, toys, sunglasses
- Water dripping down cracked windshield
- Smoke wisping from hood
- Dashboard lights dead
- Sunlight/daylight streaming in (contrast to nighttime before)

### Dialogue
*(No dialogue - discovery)*

### Sound Design
Dripping water, creaking metal, distant birds

## Panel 7D: Two-Shot - Nina Rouses Gabe
**Shot Type:** Medium two-shot | **Duration:** 20s | **Camera:** STATIC

### Composition
Nina reaching over to wake Gabe. Both visible in frame.

### Character Positions

## Panel 7F: Wide Through Window - Swamp Reveal
**Shot Type:** Wide | **Duration:** 29s | **Camera:** SLOW PAN across landscape

### Composition
Looking through car window/windshield at the JURASSIC SWAMP. Full reveal of the prehistoric world.

### Character Positions
- (Landscape shot - window frame at edges)

### Key Action
The audience's first full view of the Jurassic world. Hold for awe.

### Camera Movement
Slow pan across the swamp landscape. Let it breathe.

### Visual Details
**ENVIRONMENT:**
- Lush tropical swamp
- Giant ferns (some twice car height)
- Exotic flowers (unknown species)
- Humid atmosphere/steam rising
- Water visible between plants
- Unusual trees (cycads, tree ferns)
- Strange insects flying
- NO sign of modern civilization

**ATMOSPHERE:**
- Warm, golden-green light
- Humid/misty quality
- Dense vegetation
- Primordial beauty

### Dialogue
**GABE (O.S.):** "Where are we?"
**NINA (O.S.):** "I think we drove into the ditch."
**GABE (O.S.):** "Uhhhh... my head."
**NINA (O.S.):** "Gabe, you have to see this."

### Sound Design
Full jungle ambiance: birds, insects, water, distant animal calls
