# Scene 11: EXT. HOUSE - MORNING

**Duration:** 0:15 | **Visual Complexity:** 2 | **Panels:** 3

## Setting
Exterior of the Bornsztein family home. Morning after the parents' disappearance. Police cars in driveway. Official presence. Suburban neighborhood awakening.

## Characters
- **Police Officers** (Background): Multiple officers, vehicles
- **Official** (Brief): On phone, coordinating
- **Nosy Neighbor** (Brief): Getting newspaper, snooping

## VFX Requirements
- None (practical scene)

## Panel 11A: Wide - House Exterior
**Shot Type:** Wide establishing | **Duration:** 6s | **Camera:** STATIC

### Composition
Full view of Bornsztein house exterior. Multiple police cars in driveway. Yellow tape visible. Morning sunlight. Stark contrast to stormy night before.

### Character Positions
- Police Officers: Near cars, on porch
- Official: Visible by car, on phone
- (House dominant in frame)

### Key Action
Establishing the aftermath. Investigation is underway. The ordinary suburban home is now a scene.

### Visual Details
- 2-3 police cars, lights not flashing (investigation, not emergency)
- Yellow caution tape near driveway entrance
- Morning light: golden, calm (ironic contrast)
- House looks normal from outside
- Kids' bikes visible in yard (reminder of children)

### Dialogue
*(No dialogue - establishing shot)*

### Sound Design
Radio chatter, car doors, morning birds, distant suburban sounds
