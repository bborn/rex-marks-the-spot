# Scene 13: INT. HOUSE - DAY (CONTINUOUS)

**Duration:** 2:30 | **Visual Complexity:** 2 | **Panels:** 7

## Setting
Bornsztein family kitchen/breakfast nook. Daytime. Social worker attempting to interview the children. Leo eating sugary cereal messily. Mia more composed but determined.

## Characters
- **Mia** (~8): Older sibling, intuitive, protective, determined
- **Leo** (~5): Dinosaur pajamas, messy eater, innocent, honest
- **Social Worker**: Kind but overwhelmed, professional
- **Detective McNattin**: Background initially, takes phone at end

## VFX Requirements
- None (practical scene)

**Shot Type:** Medium to Insert | **Duration:** 30s | **Camera:** STATIC then PUSH to phone

### Camera Movement

### Visual Details

- Nina's phone (cracked screen from crash?)
- Faint glow still visible on phone

- Kids watching with concern
