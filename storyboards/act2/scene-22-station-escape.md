# Scene 22: INT. POLICE STATION - NIGHT

**Duration:** 4:00 | **Visual Complexity:** 3 | **Panels:** 12

## Setting
Police station. Late night. Ruben returns to help kids escape. Magic misfires. Slapstick comedy sequence.

## Characters
- **Mia** (~8): Problem-solver, brave
- **Leo** (~5): Eager helper, comic relief
- **Ruben** (Fairy Godfather): Rusty magic, determined
- **Detective McNattin**: Sleeping, then frozen

## VFX Requirements
- **Magic effects:** Multiple misfires (wheel disappears, chair moves, ceiling hole, freeze spell)
- **Practical effects:** Chair ramming door

- Second longest scene in Act 2 - needs comedic pacing
- Physical comedy mixed with magic failures
- Kids solve door problem themselves (not helpless)
- Ruben's one success = freeze spell (saves them)
- McNattin as comedic obstacle, not villain
- Leo poking frozen McNattin = big laugh
- Escape feels earned despite chaos
- Sets up car chase (Scene 23)
