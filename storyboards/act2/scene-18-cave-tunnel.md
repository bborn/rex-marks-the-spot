# Scene 18: INT. JURASSIC CAVE TUNNEL - DAY

**Duration:** 0:30 | **Visual Complexity:** 3 | **Panels:** 3

## Setting
Narrow cave tunnel. Parents crawling toward exit. Light at end. Signal weakening deeper in the cave.

## Characters
- **Nina** (Mother): Leading, phone in hand, watching signal
- **Gabe** (Father): Following, encouraging

## VFX Requirements
- **Phone effects:** Signal bars dropping, static increasing

---

## Panel 18A: Tracking - Parents in Tunnel
**Shot Type:** Tracking medium | **Duration:** 15s | **Camera:** TRACK alongside

### Composition
Parents moving through narrow tunnel. Single file. Nina leading with phone held forward like a torch.

### Character Positions
- Nina: Leading, screen left
- Gabe: Following close behind
- Tunnel: Tight around them

### Key Action
Physical journey matching emotional journey. Moving toward exit but losing signal.

### Camera Movement
Track alongside them, showing progress and struggle.

### Visual Details
- Narrow tunnel (have to duck/crawl in places)
- Rough rock walls
- Light ahead (getting brighter)
- Parents' ruined formal wear dragging
- Nina's bare feet on rock
- Phone held like beacon

### Dialogue
**NINA:** "Keep moving. I can see light ahead."
**GABE:** "How's the signal?"
**NINA:** "Not good. Getting weaker."

### Sound Design
Shuffling, rocks scraping, echoing voices, phone static

---

## Panel 18B: Close-up - Phone Signal Bars
**Shot Type:** Insert close-up | **Duration:** 8s | **Camera:** STATIC

### Composition
Phone screen showing signal bars dropping. The visual representation of hope fading.

### Character Positions
- (Phone only, Nina's hand visible)

### Key Action
Signal dropping. One bar. Then flickering. The connection weakening.

### Visual Details
- Signal bars: 2 bars → 1 bar → flickering
- Static increasing on screen
- Battery still showing (maybe 60% now?)
- Screen glitching more
- Nina's fingers visible, gripping tightly

### Dialogue
**NINA (O.S.):** "No, no, no... come on..."
**NINA (O.S.):** "I'm losing it, Gabe."

### Sound Design
Static increasing, signal drop sound (soft beep), Nina's frustrated voice

---

## Panel 18C: Medium - Hope Fading
**Shot Type:** Medium | **Duration:** 7s | **Camera:** STATIC

### Composition
Nina paused in tunnel, looking at phone in despair. Gabe catching up behind her. Light from exit visible ahead.

### Character Positions
- Nina: Center, stopped
- Gabe: Behind her, reaching out supportively
- Exit light: Visible behind them (hope of different kind)

### Key Action
Moment of loss. Signal gone. But physical escape route remains.

### Visual Details
- Nina's devastated face
- Phone screen mostly static
- Gabe's hand on her shoulder
- Tunnel exit light behind them
- Transition from hope (phone) to determination (escape)

### Dialogue
**NINA:** "It's gone. I've lost them."
**GABE:** "We'll find another way. We have to get out of here first."
**NINA:** "What if that was our only chance?"
**GABE:** "It wasn't. The phone still has power. The portal is still out there."

### Sound Design
Static dying out, cave dripping, wind from exit, emotional silence

- Signal loss is emotional gut-punch
- But exit light represents alternative hope
- Gabe's optimism keeps Nina going
- Phone power remaining = story continues
- Sets up the montage (Scene 19) - they must keep moving
- Visual transition: blue phone glow to golden exit light
- Loss of connection motivates entire rest of Act 2
