# Scene 23: EXT. STREETS - NIGHT

**Duration:** 2:00 | **Visual Complexity:** 4 | **Panels:** 8

## Setting
City streets at night. Police parking lot, then streets. Car chase with Mia driving, Leo on her lap. Comedic action sequence.

## Characters
- **Mia** (~8): Driving (!), determined, scared but brave
- **Leo** (~5): On Mia's lap, "helping" steer
- **Ruben** (Fairy Godfather): Passenger seat, using magic to operate car
- **Police** (Background): Pursuing

## VFX Requirements
- **Magic effects:** Mop starting ignition, possible steering assistance
- **Phone effects:** Signal improving as they get closer

- Ruben is comic horror

- Police lights: Red/blue

### Mood Board Keywords
- Absurdist action
- Comedy chase

- Chase should be funny, not scary
- Kids driving = absurd, not dangerous

- Sets up Scene 25 (finding the portal)
- Ruben's magic keeping car going = team effort
- Leo "helping" steer = comedy gold
