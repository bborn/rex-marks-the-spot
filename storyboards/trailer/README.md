## Production Notes

### Key Moments for Marketing
1. **Hook moment:** Mia's "Promise?" face (003)
2. **VFX showcase:** Time warp portal (005)
3. **Monster reveal:** T-Rex (011)
4. **Comedy gold:** Kids in police car (014)
5. **Signature creature:** Jetplane intro (016)
6. **Unique gag:** Colored farts (017)
7. **Climax tease:** Giant Jetplane (023)
8. **Heart:** Family reunion (024)

### Character Consistency Notes
- Mia: Brown hair, big eyes, protective
- Leo: Light hair, round face, dinosaur obsessed
- Gabe: Dark hair, stubble, tuxedo → dirty
- Nina: Brown hair, elegant dress → ruined
- Ruben: Wild gray hair, janitor clothes, mop
- Jetplane: Chicken-lizard hybrid, cute, colorful
